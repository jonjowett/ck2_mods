[h1]Summary:[/h1]
This mod allows players additional customisation options when creating a [url=https://ck2.paradoxwikis.com/Empires#Custom_empires]custom empire[/url]:
[list]
[*]Coat of arms and map colour:
[list]
[*]You can use the CoA and map colour of your primary title, as per the vanilla decision.
[*]Alternatively, you can use the CoA and map colour of any county/duchy/kingdom title in your realm.
[/list]
[*]All completely-owned kingdoms will become de jure part of the new empire.
[*]Unowned baronies (eg. owned by independent holy orders) have no impact on any part of the decision.
[/list]

[h1]Compatibility/Impact:[/h1]
The aim of the "Expanded Decisions" mod family is to be as compatible as possible with vanilla CK2 (and other mods), and to avoid making the game easier.
[list]
[*]Required CK2 version: 3.3.5.1
[*]Achievement compatible: No
[*]Safe to add to existing game: Yes
[*]Safe to remove from existing game: Yes
[*]Changes to vanilla files: None
[*]Mod compatibility: Should be compatible with all other mods
[*]Changes to AI behaviour: Negligible (AI rarely form custom empires)
[*]Decrease in game difficulty: Negligible
[*]Localisation: English only
[*]Mod development status: Complete
[/list]
